Mission

To teach students with practical skills in programming, design, and filmmaking while cultivating independent thinking, and curiosity. We help learners break free from passive consumption and become creators.

"A teacher doesn't just give answers — they light fires." — Nika Keshelava

Philosophy

We believe education must balance human intuition with modern tools. Students must struggle, fail, and iterate — those experiences build true problem-solving capacity that AI cannot replace.

  • Full course of programming
  • Monthly projects and hackathons
  • Community-driven growth and mentorship

Community

GOA is more than classes — it's a network. Students meet, work on hackathons, and leave with both skills and friendships. Parents and mentors regularly participate in showcases.
